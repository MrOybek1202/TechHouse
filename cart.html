Honor X8b 8/256GB - Innovation, Style, and Performance

Elegance and Comfort

This smartphone impresses with its modern performance and ultra-thin body measuring just 6.78 mm in thickness. Lightweight components ensure ease of use, while the ergonomic design fits perfectly in hand. The 6.7-inch AMOLED display matrix provides saturated colors, high contrast, and smooth animation thanks to the 90 Hz refresh rate. The 20.1:9 aspect ratio creates ideal conditions for video watching, text work, and interface interaction.

High Power and Speed

The eight-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 680 processor with 2.4 GHz frequency is responsible for the device's efficient performance. The Adreno 610 graphics accelerator ensures processing of modern games and high-definition videos. Thanks to 8 GB of fast memory, launching and switching between apps happens instantly, while 256 GB of storage allows storing large files without constantly deleting data.

Professional-Level Photos

The main camera consists of three modules: a 108-megapixel sensor for clear and detailed shots, a 5-megapixel ultra-wide-angle lens for panoramic scenes, and a 2-megapixel macro lens for close-up photography. The 50 MP front camera creates quality selfies, providing natural color reproduction and accurate focusing. Built-in image processing algorithms automatically enhance images, making them more expressive.

Long Battery Life and Instant Charging

The 4500 mAh battery allows all-day active use of the gadget. Support for fast charging technology reduces energy replenishment time, which is especially important in a busy life rhythm. Energy-saving functions help extend the device's runtime by optimizing resource usage.

Modern Technologies and Security

The device supports NFC, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi networks, allowing easy synchronization with other devices and contactless payments. Built-in GPS, GLONASS, Beidou, and Galileo navigation modules provide high accuracy in determining coordinates. The side-mounted fingerprint scanner instantly unlocks the screen to ensure personal data security. The Android 13 operating system offers a convenient interface and a wide range of features for comfortable daily task completion.
